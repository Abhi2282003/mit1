LLB

Programme Overview

The LLB (Bachelor of Laws) is a three-year programme that provides students with a thorough understanding of the legal system, including business laws, intellectual property rights, alternative dispute resolution, and the Indian Constitution.
Students gain insight into various acts and laws through seminars with academicians, industry experts, and practicing lawyers.
The School of Law has a dedicated Employability, Placement, and Career Advancement Cell to help students secure placements and other career opportunities as budding lawyers.
