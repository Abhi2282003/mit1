BBA LL.B. ( Hons)

Programme Overview

The BBA LLB is a five-year comprehensive programme that combines the study of management with law. The programme provides practical training in law and a strong foundation in management, finance, marketing, and human resources. This combination prepares students for successful careers in business and litigation. During the final semester, students have regular interactions with management and litigation experts, participate in moot court exercises, and complete internships.
