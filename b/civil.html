B.Tech Civil Engineering

Programme Overview

The B.Tech Civil Engineering in Smart Infrastructure and Construction programme at MIT-WPU prepares students to construct innovative infrastructures by combining physical infrastructure assets and construction processes with digital technologies, also known as smart technologies.
For effective infrastructure management, students learn to use smart technologies such as sensors and citizen science, actuators, data transmission, the internet of things, big data analytics, data visualization, and blockchain.
Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (AI & ML), data science, dimensional building modeling and simulation, drone technology, and other topics are covered in the course curriculum.
